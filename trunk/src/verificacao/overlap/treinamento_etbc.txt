Obs:
	-comando "cds" é para registrar o comando para executar o irun (programa da cadence)
	-comando "unset ETBC_TLN_DIR" desregistra diretório default para o .tln
	

Antes das prátiacs começarem devemos definir nosso arquivo .tln
Um exemplo desse arquivo está na pasta 	UEFS/treinamento_m/pratica1.1/overlap.tln
	

Pratica 1.1 (necessário ter o arquivo *.tln):

mkdir pratica1.1
cds
unset ETBC_TLN_DIR
etbc overlap.tln trans overlap
etbc overlap.tln sdi overlap
etbc overlap.tln pre_source overlap
etbc overlap.tln sink overlap
etbc overlap.tln refmod overlap
etbc overlap.tln tb_single_refmod overlap
etbc overlap.tln tb_tcl overlap
etbc overlap.tln Makefile_single_refmod overlap
make
(qualquer problema "make clean" antes de fazer make novamente)
